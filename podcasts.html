Office Ladies [En]

If you like The Office, you will really enjoy Angela Kingsley and Jenna Fischer breaking down each and every single episode of the show.

They are two incredibly and lovely people, and have a very strong connection, so that they know how to talk together and that is really amusing.

The Partially Examined Life [En]

The Partially Examined Life is a show about philosophy for people that do not want to read. Or for people that want to read philosophy but don't know what to read now.

The people involved are Mark Linsenmayer, Seth Paskin, Wes Alwan and (most recently) Dylan Casey. They basically discuss books and is really interesting and funny.

This is actualy the podcast that made go go into podcasts, and I find that interesting since it is a long format podcast, and often this format is not really easy to listen.

But oh my god, Dan Carlin is incredible. He made different voices for different characters! It is just great.

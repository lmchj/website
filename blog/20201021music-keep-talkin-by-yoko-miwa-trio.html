Music: Keep Talkin' by Yoko Miwa Trio

This is an album that I have had on my head for a lot of time. I think I first listened to it on July or something like that.

It is just incredible!

Yoko Miwa is an incredibly talented pianist. She and Connie Han—whose album Iron Starlett I will review later—are my favourite jazz pianists right now. This album feels fresh and soothing but also it is full of energy, I'm just in love with her music.

I have to admit that I hate the pop-jazz shitty covers she make. I mean she covers The Beatles, what kind of stupid joke is that. But the songs from herself are just amazing.

  • Favourite songs: Keep Talkin', If You Are Blue, Sunshine Follows The Rain and her cover of Booggie Stop Shuffle
  • Less favourite song: Golden Slumbers/You Never Give Me Your Money. I mean, The Beatles? You are way better than this shit.

Anyway, I am not the critique about anything, I just enjoy things. Listen to it and let Yoko Miwa hug you.
